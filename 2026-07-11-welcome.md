Welcome! This blog exists to help you get up to speed quickly, whether you're brand new to the lab or just new to a specific piece of hardware or software we use.

## What you'll find here

- Step-by-step tutorials
- Video walkthroughs
- Troubleshooting notes from real issues we've run into

## How to use this site

Posts are tagged by topic (like "Getting Started" or "Motion Control") so you can jump straight to what's relevant. Newest posts show up first on the home page.

Here's an example of embedding a video in a post:

<iframe width="560" height="315" src="https://www.youtube.com/embed/dQw4w9WgXcQ" frameborder="0" allowfullscreen></iframe>

Just replace the video ID in that link with your own YouTube video's ID (the part after `v=` in a normal YouTube URL).
